Dong LI received a BEng degree in Computer Science in 2006, a MEng degree in Computer Science in 2009, both from Tianjin University, Tianjin, and a PhD degree in 2014 from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong SAR, China. Currently, he is an Assistant Professor in Guangdong University of Technology. His research interests include topics in the fields of computer vision, pattern recognition, and image processing, such as feature matching, face recognition, and color correction.
